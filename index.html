About Us

Our Mission The Environment Conservation Group (ECG) is dedicated to protecting and restoring the natural environment in and around Coimbatore and the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. Our mission is to foster a deeper connection between people and nature while advocating for the conservation of endangered species and the preservation of critical ecosystems.

Our Vision A world where human development coexists harmoniously with nature, ensuring the protection of biodiversity, clean air, water, and a stable climate for all life forms.

Our Story Founded in 2010, ECG was established by a group of passionate conservationists who were deeply concerned about the rapid degradation of the natural environment. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted voice for environmental advocacy in Tamil Nadu, working closely with local communities, schools, and governments to create meaningful change.

Our Projects

Vulture Conservation India’s vulture population has faced a steep decline in recent decades, and ECG is actively working to protect these critically endangered birds. Our vulture conservation project focuses on habitat protection, awareness campaigns, and collaboration with government bodies to regulate harmful substances.

Habitat Restoration Through our reforestation efforts and land restoration projects, we aim to revive degraded ecosystems in and around Coimbatore. We organize regular tree-planting drives and engage with communities to help restore natural habitats vital for the survival of local wildlife.

Wildlife Protection The Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve is home to a diverse array of species, including elephants, tigers, and leopards. ECG plays a critical role in monitoring wildlife populations and preventing human-wildlife conflicts. Our wildlife conservation team works on protecting endangered species through research, community engagement, and conservation measures.

Environmental Awareness We believe that education is the foundation of long-term change. ECG regularly conducts workshops, seminars, and school programs to raise awareness about the importance of environmental conservation, climate change, and sustainability. By empowering future generations, we aim to cultivate a culture of environmental stewardship.
